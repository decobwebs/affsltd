Stevedoring

We provide professional stevedoring services with dedicated personnel for port and terminal operations, along with general maritime services, on/offshore hire, cargo pre-shipment surveys, and thorough inspections of containers and barges.

Lifting and Hoisting

We provide reliable lifting and hoisting solutions with competent personnel, handling both complex and non-complex lifts using certified equipment and accessories to ensure safety, efficiency, and compliance in every operation.

Cargo Handling

We specialize in comprehensive cargo handling solutions, ensuring efficiency, safety, and compliance with global standards. Our services cover containerized cargo, break bulk, cargo consolidation, bulk liquid and dry cargoes, chartered vessel operations, and the transportation of heavy and over-dimension vehicles. With a commitment to excellence, we optimize logistics and stevedoring operations to deliver seamless and reliable freight solutions.

Cargo Characteristics

We handle a wide range of cargo with expertise, ensuring safe and efficient transport across all operations. From containerized shipments to bulk liquids like petroleum products and dry commodities such as cement, wheat, and fertilizer, we manage every load with precision. Our services extend to bagged goods, heavy lift cargo, hydro pipes, consolidated shipments, and commercial items, ensuring seamless logistics for export produce, vehicles, motorcycles, and intermodal transport. With a strong focus on safety, compliance, and efficiency, we deliver reliable solutions tailored to industry demands.
